  Projects:

    Scratch:

      I created multiple projects in Scratch, during Girls Who Code, but the final project was the legitimate one. We created
      a side scrolling game where the sprites/characters weren't moving but the back drop was. Many popular games today use
      this method, such as Sonic or Mario. My team's game took a different take on the idea and decided instead of having the
      character 'running somewhere, we can make it more simple by having the items falling. The game was called Mary Moo's and
      the objective was to catch more ice cream scoops than the opposing player in 60 seconds.
